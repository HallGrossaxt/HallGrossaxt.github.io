Of Dice & Heroes

A workshop for fantasy roleplaying tools, kept beside the laboratory.
❦ ✦ ❦

Herein lie the works concerning matters fantastical — small tools for the gaming table, written in the hours when the laboratory grows quiet. The first volume in this folio shall be a character generator and manager for Midgard 5; further entries are promised as time and inspiration permit. All works are kept in earnest, polished slowly, and offered freely upon completion.
